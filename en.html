Chern Class, School of Mathematical Sciences, Nankai University · Undergraduate research at the College of Computer Science

I was born in 2005 in Hengdong, Hunan. I am an undergraduate student in the Chern Class (Class of 2023) at the School of Mathematical Sciences, Nankai University, and currently conduct research at the College of Computer Science under the supervision of Prof. Qing Guo.

I graduated from Zhonghua High School in Nanjing in 2023, with a perfect physics score in that year's Jiangsu Gaokao. Outside of academics, I enjoy running, badminton, and hiking.

Within my class, I have served as class monitor, League branch secretary, academic representative, and wellness officer.
